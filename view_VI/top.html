Start Diagnosis

How to use is easy. Please choose your question choice, such as how to use your home life and energy. You do not mind skipping more things you do not know. Conversely, if you want to make a detailed diagnosis, you can set more detailed information such as "by category" or "detailed version".

Depending on the answer, the result will change more and more. It will be shown in a graph such as what is more or less than the average, what kind of equipment is a problem, please refer to it. The order of effective countermeasures will also change.

The displayed countermeasures are arranged in the recommended order. Please check and select something that you are willing to work on even if you are not right now, and interest you. When measures are taken, it is immediately possible to know the simulation result as to how much utility expenses and CO2 emissions of the house are reduced.
