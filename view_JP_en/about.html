About how to use energy saving diagnosis

Anyone can use this energy-saving diagnosis free of charge, but it does not guarantee the accuracy of the results. Please use it as a hint to promote energy conservation.

You can not use the back button of the browser.

privacy policy
  • Since this diagnostic software is executed by downloading the calculation logic itself to the terminal, the entered value is never sent to other than the user terminal.
  • This page uses Google Analitics to understand usage situation.

Recommended diagnostic screen

Although it is the same diagnostic content, there are three kinds of designs. If you want to easily tackle " simplified version ", if you want to see the outline of diagnosis, " List screen version is recommended. Also, if you want to make a detailed detailed diagnosis, detailed version is also available. In any case, you can proceed to more detailed diagnosis while taking over the data you filled in.

Content of diagnosis result

  • Countermeasures: Approximately 70 Items Calculate prepared countermeasures and select effective CO2 reduction measures for your home. We will show not only the CO2 reduction amount but also the reduction amount of the utility fee, so please refer to thinking about our efforts. In the list screen version and detailed version, you can choose a combination of countermeasures and can simulate how much reduction can be done at home. You also get measure list.
  • Comparison: You can compare whether the energy consumption is high or low, compared with the same family number, local family. If there are 100 households in a similar environment, it also shows how many.
  • Breakdown: We estimate what is the main cause of CO2 emissions in our household. For many applications, it is effective to take measures.

Technical explanation on energy saving diagnosis

This diagnosis is provided by Hinode and Hitachi Eco Life Institute (Hinodeya Institute for Ecolife co.). It is a review of the calculation logic of 'Eko diagnosis' that is currently being advanced by the Ministry of the Environment from the basics, and it is designed to be compatible with flexible systems such as multilingual.

Effective measures will vary depending on the energy use situation of home and office. By answering within the range to understand, it is possible to analyze and suggest countermeasures with more families situation. However, you do not need to forcibly answer items you do not know. Through logic that guesses the usage situation, appropriate measures are being proposed without having to forcibly answer.

We propose a motion model, and we are calculating approximate numerical values ​​according to input for the proposed contents, but there is no guarantee of accuracy or operation. If inappropriate suggestions or numbers are output, we would be pleased if you could contact us. We will respond to development separately for systems that have reviewed the diagnostic logic.
